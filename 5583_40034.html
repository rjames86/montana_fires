Moose Peak and Miller Fires

Multi-Jurisdictional Area Closure Order

The USDA Forest Service and Weyerhaeuser jointly restrict public access to lands under their jurisdiction described within this order. The closure cited in this order is to protect the public from potential harm from wildfires and firefighting operations associated with the Moose Peak and Miller Fires.

This closure order is effective upon signing and is in effect until rescinded. Periodic assessments will be conducted to evaluate closure needs.

USDA Forest Service

Pursuant to Title 36 Code of Federal Regulations 261.50(a), the following act is prohibited as prescribed below within the Plains/Thompson Falls Ranger District of the Lolo National Forest. These restrictions are in addition to those enumerated in Subpart A, Part 261, Title 36, Code of Federal Regulations, and become effective upon signing and will remain in effect until rescinded.

36 CFR – Special Closures

It is prohibited to go into or be upon the area (36 CFR 261.53(e)).

The closure cited in this order is to protect the public from potential harm from wildfires and firefighting operation associate with the Moose Peak and Miller Fires.

Description of Closed Areas

On National Forest System lands, this order closes the following area (see-attached map):

· The Fishtrap Drainage and its tributaries from the confluence of Beatrice Creek and Fishtrap Creek north to the common boundary between the Lolo and Kootenai National Forests, east to the Forest Boundary, and west to the common boundary between the Lolo and Kootenai National Forests generally including the following tributary drainages:

o Young Creek

o West Fork Fishtrap Creek, including the West Fork Fishtrap Creek Campground

o Whiteface Creek

o Beartrap Creek

o Cool Creek

o Radio Creek, including Fishtrap Lake and the Fishtrap Lake Campground

o Mantrap Creek

o Daisy Creek

o Little Creek

o Shorthorn Gulch

o Shale Creek

It is prohibited to be on roads (36 CFR 261.54(e)); trails (36 CFR 261.55(a)), and campgrounds within the closure perimeter.

Exemptions

Pursuant to 36 CFR 261.50(e), the following persons are exempt from this order:

1. Persons with a permit specifically authorizing the otherwise prohibited act or omission.

2. Any Federal, State, or local officer, or member of an organized rescue or firefighting force in the performance of an official duty.

Penalty

Violation of these prohibitions is punishable by a fine of not more than $5000 for an individual, or $10,000 for an organization, or imprisonment for not more than 6 months, or both (16 USC 551 and 18, USC 3559 and 3571).

Notification

A copy of this order shall be posted as prescribed in 36 CFR 261.51.

Sanders County, Montana DNRC, Weyerhaeuser

Sanders County, Montana DNRC, and Weyerhaeuser close the lands under their jurisdiction within the perimeter identified within this order except for privately owned land in the following locations Sections 8 and 18, T24N, R27W, Section 32, T25N, R27W, and Section 1, T25N, R28W.

Signed in Missoula, Montana, this 4th day of September 2017.
