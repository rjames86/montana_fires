08-31-2017_Sheep Gap Fire Update

  • By Ryan M
  • September 01, 2017 at 13:50:00
August 31, 2017, 7 P.M. Contact: John Hamilton Phone: 406-826-4345 or 406-529-5475 Inciweb: https://inciweb.nwcg.gov/incident/5558/ What: Sheep Gap Fire Update Location: Fire is approximately 9 miles west of Plains, Plains-Thompson Falls Ranger District (T 20 North R 27 W Section 7) Size: Approximately 1000 acres Containment: 0 Cause: Started by lightning at approximately 5 p.m., Aug. 29 The Sheep Gap Fire: Fire was started by lightning on the evening of Aug. 29 and quickly grew to over 200 acres that night. By evening Aug 30, the fire has grown to an estimated 1000 acres. The Forest Service will conduct an infrared detection flight overnight to determine the new size and perimeter of the fire. Today firefighters reinforced fire line on the northern edge to prevent movement toward structures and other resources of value.
