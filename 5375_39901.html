Smoke Outlook for September 3, 2017

  • View Fire Info
  • September 03, 2017 at 16:42:00
Smoke: Air Quality Alert in effect today for Missoula, Lake,and Ravalli counties. Smoke will persist this morning in theBitterroot Valley, lifting some this afternoon. Smoke willreturn in areas closest to active fires this afternoon as fireactivity increases; smoke will become much morewidespread overnight. Fire: Extremely hot and dry conditions will persist for onemore day across the Northern Rockies. These dryconditions will combine with breezy westerly winds and anunstable atmosphere this afternoon and evening to createcritical fire weather conditions across western Montana. Other: Winds will switch by tomorrow from the west to theeast, creating problematic fire weather conditions tonightinto Monday. High pressure will return by Wednesday,bring back hot and dry conditions, conducive to stagnantair and smoky skies. If you live within Missoula County,please refer to the Missoula City-County Health Departmentfor detailed smoke response information.
