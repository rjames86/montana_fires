Liberty Fire Emergency Area Closure Sept. 1, 2017

  • By Ryan M
  • September 01, 2017 at 13:39:00
UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE FOREST SERVICE LOLO NATIONAL FOREST BUREAU OF LAND MANAGEMENT MISSOULA FIELD OFFICE THE NATURE CONSERVANCY Liberty Fire Emergency Area Closure PROHIBITIONS: Pursuant to 36 CFR 261.50 (b) and 43 CFR 9212.2(a) (b) (1) the following act is prohibited on the area, roads, and trails as described in this order until further notice: Going into or being upon the fire area as described below and depicted on the attached map that is closed for the protection of public health or safety. Nature Conservancy - Criminal Trespass to property (MCA 45-6-203). US Forest Service - 36 CFR 261.53(e) Bureau of Land Management - 43 CFR 9212.1(g) EXEMPTIONS: The following persons are exempt from this order: Persons with a permit specifically exempting them from the effect of this Order.
