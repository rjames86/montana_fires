Daily update August 30, 2017 (Stone Lake Fire Wildfire)

  • By Ryan M
  • August 31, 2017 at 14:27:00
FIRE UPDATE Stone Lake Fire August 31, 2017 10:00 a.m. Contact: Wisdom Ranger District (406) 689-3243 M-F 8:00 – 4:30 What: Wildland Fire Detected: August 27, 2017, 2:00 p.m. Cause: Lightning Current Size: Approximately 8 acres Containment: 70% Resources on Fire: 5 Smokejumpers and local 6 Wisdom/Wise River firefighters Location: N 45˚41.865 W 113˚16.230 Approximately 15 miles SW of Wise River, MT The Stone Lake fire was detected at aproximately 2:00 p.m. on Sunday, August 27, 2017. This fire Is located high on the ridge in Whitebark Pine with rock screes nearby. Today firefighters will continue to mop up, scout and extinguish all spots outside of the main fire perimeter. Water bucket drops will be available if needed and visibility allows for arial support.
