Air Quality Report for 9-1-2017 (Rice Ridge Fire Wildfire)

  • By Ryan M
  • September 01, 2017 at 10:21:00
Smoke: The hot, stagnant conditions from the last few days will give way to critical fire weather today for dry thunderstorms with gusty outflow winds. This will be followed by a dry cold front that should pass through the area beginning this evening bringing increased westerly winds. Active fire behavior, with the potential for rapid growth is possible. Yesterday, Rice Ridge and Liberty added 1,560 acres and 529 acres respectively. Fire: As the stable conditions break down there should be a slight clearing of smoke later today and into the evening, however this will be slow to materialize and variable in terms of the locations due to the erratic nature of the winds associated the dry thunderstorms. As the cold front moves through this evening and tomorrow, accumulated smoke should start to clear but this could be followed by new smoke.
