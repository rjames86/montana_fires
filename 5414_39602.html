Air Quality Report for 9-1-2017

Incident: Rice Ridge Fire Wildfire
Released: 1 day ago

Related Information

  • Air Quality Report for 9-1-2017 (pdf 163 kb)

Smoke: The hot, stagnant conditions from the last few days will give way to critical fire weather today for dry thunderstorms with gusty outflow winds. This will be followed by a dry cold front that should pass through the area beginning this evening bringing increased westerly winds. Active fire behavior, with the potential for rapid growth is possible. Yesterday, Rice Ridge and Liberty added 1,560 acres and 529 acres respectively.

Fire: As the stable conditions break down there should be a slight clearing of smoke later today and into the evening, however this will be slow to materialize and variable in terms of the locations due to the erratic nature of the winds associated the dry thunderstorms. As the cold front moves through this evening and tomorrow, accumulated smoke should start to clear but this could be followed by new smoke. Seeley Lake will remain Hazardous and the rest of the sites will remain in the Unhealthy to USG range.

Other: Critical fire weather will continue Thursday with sustained dry gusty winds. For additional information please refer to Missoula City-County Health Department in the "links" below.
