Red Flag Warning in Effect for Dry fuels and Strong Gusty Winds

  • View Fire Info
  • September 03, 2017 at 08:44:00
A Red Flag Warning for dry fuels and strong gusty winds is in effect beginning Sunday morning, through Monday Evening. A Red Flag Warning is a condition where fire activity is expected to increase. The fire triangle consist of Fuels, Weather and Terrain - when these parts of the triangle align is a negative a manner, fire activity increases. In this case, the forecasted weather is for strong gusty winds and the fuels in the fire area are extremely dry. Add the rolling and steep terrain that is in the area of the fire, and elements are aligned to promote active fire
