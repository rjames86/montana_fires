Lolo National Forest Closure Order F17-064-LOLO-D3 & D6 Amended 3, 9/2/17

Incident: Liberty Fire Wildfire
Released: 5 hrs. ago

LOLO NATIONAL FOREST
24 Fort Missoula Road
Missoula, Montana 59804-7297
FOREST SUPERVISOR’S ORDER

Pursuant to Title 36 Code of Federal Regulations 261.50 (a & b), the following acts are prohibited as described below within the Missoula and Seeley Lake Ranger Districts of the Lolo National Forest. These restrictions are in addition to those enumerated in Subpart A, Part 261, Title 36, Code of Federal Regulations, and become effective upon signing and will remain in effect until rescinded.

36 CFR – Special Closures

It is prohibited to go into or be upon the area (36 CFR 261.53(e)).

The closure cited in this order is to protect the public from potential harm from wildfires and firefighting operations associated with the Liberty Fire.

Description of Closed Areas:

This area closure applies to the Lolo National Forest and closes the area as described below:

All of sections 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, & 12, T. 15 N., R. 16 W.

All of sections 7, 8, 12, 14, 16, 17, & 18, and parts of sections 2 – 6, 9 – 11, & 20, T. 15 N., R. 17 W.

All of section 12 and parts of sections 1 – 3, 10, 11, 13, & 14, T. 15 N., R. 18 W.

All of sections 20, 28, & 32, and parts of sections 7, 18, 29, 30, & 34, T. 16 N., R. 16 W.

All of sections 35 & 36, and parts of sections 3, 10 – 14, 25 – 27, & 34, T. 16 N., R. 17 W.

As shown on the ATTACHED MAP.

36 CFR – National Forest System Roads

Being on the road. 36 CFR 261.54(e). It is prohibited to be on the road.

The closure cited in this order is to protect the public from potential harm from wildfires and firefighting operations associated with the Liberty Fire.

Description of Closed Road:

Seeley Lake Ranger District

Beaver Finley Road, NFSR #9974-2, from junction with Placid Creek Road, NFSR #349, west to its terminus. Grouse Creek Road, NFSR #4342, from junction with 4349 to terminus. Second Creek Road, NFSR #4346, from junction with Placid Creek Road, NFSR #349 to terminus. As shown on the ATTACHED MAP.

Including all roads originating from closed roads listed above. See description of closed roads above.

Missoula Ranger District

Gold Creek Road, NFSR #126, from its junction with Mineral Peak Road, NFSR #2112 to its terminus. Roads #2103, 4341, 4224, 2118, 2121, 16859, 4323

Including all roads originating from closed roads listed above. See description of closed roads above.

36 CFR – National Forest System Trails

Being on the trail. 36 CFR 261.55(a). It is prohibited to be on the trail.

The closure cited in this order is to protect the public from potential harm from wildfires and firefighting operations associated with the Liberty Fire.

Description of Trails:

Missoula Ranger District

Trail #504 (Porcupine), from its junction with Trail 515 (Rattlesnake) to its terminus. Trail #52, from its junction with Trail #515 (Rattlesnake) to its terminus.Trail #333, from the West Fork Gold Creek Trailhead to its terminus. Trail #518, from the Gold Creek Trailhead to its terminus. Trails #336, #334, & #519.

Including all trails encompassed by the area closure. See description of area closure above. See attached map.

Exemptions:

Pursuant to 36 CFR 261.50(e), the following persons are exempt from this order:

1. Persons with a permit issued by a forest officer specifically authorizing the otherwise prohibited act or omission.

2. Any Federal, State, or local officer, or member of an organized rescue or firefighting force in the performance of an official duty.

Signed in Missoula, Montana, this 1st day of September, 2017.

/s/ Beverly Yelczyn acting for

Timothy Garcia

Forest Supervisor

Lolo National Forest

Penalty: Violation of these prohibitions is punishable by a fine of not more than $5000 for and individual or $10,000 for an organization, or imprisonment for not more than 6 months, or both (16 USC 551 and 18 USC 3559 and 3571).

Notification: A copy of this order shall be posted as prescribed in 36 CFR 261.51.
