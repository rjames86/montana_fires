It's not worth the view. If you fly, we can't!

  • View Fire Info
  • September 09, 2017 at 18:31:00
At approximately 4:15pm today, a concerned citizen of Seeley Lake called the Rice Ridge public information line to report a drone incursion on the Rice Ridge Fire. The sighting was reported off of Cabin Lane which is accessed off of Boy Scout Road. Boy Scout Road is located west of Highway 83 and loops around the west side of Seeley Lake. We did not have air support in the area at the time so the incursion did not affect air operations. The report has been forwarded to the Missoula County Sheriff’s office and is currently under investigation.
