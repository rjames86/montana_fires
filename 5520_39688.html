9-1-17 Trail Closures due to Scalp Fire, Order #D04-151-S-17

Incident: Scalp Fire Wildfire
Released: 19 hrs. ago

D04-151-S-17


FLATHEAD NATIONAL FOREST

650 Wolfpack Way

Kalispell, MT 59901

Forest Supervisor’s Order



TRAIL RESTRICTION ORDER

Switchback Pass Trail #177, Clack Creek Trail #160, Clack Trilobite Trail #346, Basin Creek North Trail #378, Sun River Pass Trail #116, Mount May Trail #256, Trail – Mount May Connect Trail #479, Bowl Creek Trail #324, Bowl Creek Cutoff Trail #324.1, and a portion of Chair Mountain Trilobites Trail #241, are closed due to fire behavior.



Pursuant to Title 36 Code of Federal Regulation 261.50 (b), the following restriction is in effect on the Spotted Bear Ranger District on the Flathead National Forest, Flathead County, Montana. This temporary restriction is in addition to those enumerated in Subpart A, Part 261, Title 36, Code of Federal Regulations. These restrictions become effective when signed and will remain in effect until rescinded. See the attached map, Exhibit A.



36 CFR 261.55 – Forest Development Trails:



Being on the trail [Title 36 CFR 261.55(a)]

The below listed National Forest System Trail located on the Spotted Bear Ranger District is closed to all public travel because of wildfire activity associated with the Scalp Fire.



Description of Restricted National Forest Access

Trail #177, in its entirety, from the junction with Dolly Varden Pentagon Trail #173 (SW ¼ of Section 27, R 12W, T 25N) to the junction with Chair Mountain/Trilobite Trail #241 and Kevan Mountain Trail #732 (NW ¼ of Section 24, R 12W, T 25N).


Trail #160, in its entirety, from the junction with Chair Mountain/Trilobite Trail #241(NE ¼ of Section 3, R 12W, T 25N) to the junction with Big River Trail #155 (SW ¼ of Section 18, R 11W, T 26N).


Trail #346, in its entirety, from the junction with Clack Creek Trail #160 (SE ¼ of Section 23, R 12W, T 26N) to the junction with Chair Mountain/Trilobite Trail #241 (NW ¼ of Section 27, R 12W, T 26N).


Trail #378, in its entirety, from the junction with Sun River Pass Trail #116 (SW ¼ of Section 35, R 11W, T 26N) to its terminus at NE ¼ of Section 7, R 11W, T 25N.


Trail #116, in its entirety, from the junction with Bowl Creek Trail #324 (NW ¼ of Section 35, R 11W, T 26N) to Sun River Pass (NE ¼ of Section 2, R 11W, T 25N).


Trail #256, in its entirety, from the junction with Bowl Creek Trail #324 (SW ¼ of Section 20, R 11W, T 26N) to the junction with Bowl Creek Trail #324 (SW ¼ of Section 27, R 11W, T 26N).


Trail #479, in its entirety, from the junction with Mount May Trail #256 (NE ¼ of Section 27, R 11W, T 26N) to the junction with Trail Creek #216 (NW ¼ of Section 23, R 11W, T 26N).


Trail #324, in its entirety, from the junction with Strawberry Creek Trail #161 (NE ¼ of Section 20, R 11W, T 26N) to Teton Pass (SE ¼ of Section 28, R 10W, T 26N).


Trail #324.1, in its entirety, from the junction with Bowl Creek Trail #324 (NE ¼ of Section 35, R 11W, T 26N) to the junction with Sun River Pass Trail #116 (NE ¼ of Section 35, R 11W, T 26N).


Trail #241, from the junction with Clack-Trilobite Trail #346 (NW ¼ of Section 27, R 12W, T 26N) to the junction with Switchback Pass Trail #177 and Kevan Mountain Trail #732 (NW ¼ of Section 24, R 12W, T 25N).



Exemptions:

Pursuant to Title 36 CFR 261.50(e), the following are exempt from this restriction:

1. Persons with a permit issued by a forest officer specifically authorizing the otherwise prohibited act or omission.

2. Any Federal, State, or local officer, or member of an organized rescue or fire fighting force in the performance of an official duty.




Done at Kalispell, Montana this _____day of _____________, 2017.




By: ____________________________

CHIP WEBER

Forest Supervisor



Penalty: Violation of these prohibitions is punishable by a fine of not more than $5,000 for an individual or $10,000 for an organization, or imprisonment for not more than 6 months, or both (16 USC 551 and 18 USC 3559 and 3571).


Notification: A copy of this order shall be posted as prescribed in 36 CFR 261.51.
