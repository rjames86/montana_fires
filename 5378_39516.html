Much of the forecast area will remain “Moderate” for Thursday, Missoula is the exception at"USG". Smoke is expected to filter back into valley locations overnight and remain through Friday morning.Fire: The Sunrise fire is located 11 miles SE of Superior, MT adjacent to I-90. A red flag warning is in effect. Minimalfire growth observed on the fire Wednesday. Increase in acreage is not expected on the Sunrise fire.Other: A Red Flag Warning for potential new lightning starts and gusty winds is in effect until 9pm tonight. Breezywest winds today following a weather disturbance bringing slightly cooler temperatures. Very hot and dry critical fireweather conditions remain. Expect an increase in fire behavior.
